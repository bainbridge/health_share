Core Analytical Principles

Reproducibility

  • End-to-end reproducibility is a fundamental necessity in any analysis.
  • The steps to gather, transform and model data should always be clearly annotated and tested to ensure that results are consistent and able to be peer-reviewed.

    • Interpretability

  • It is not enough to simply aggregate data and display summaries for viewers to digest.
  • Valuable anlysis is complete from problem statement to conclusion and provides viewers with recommendations backed by statistically sound methods in a clearly communicated fashion.

    • Automatability

  • The benefits of reproducibility, consistency and cost savings make automation efforts crucial in today's business environment.
  • Up-front investments to create the infrastructure needed for automated processes are well worth it and should be a priority.
